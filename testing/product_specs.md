# Product Specifications

## Discount Codes
- SAVE10 → 10% discount
- SAVE20 → 20% discount
- Codes must be applied using the "Apply Coupon" button.

## Shipping Charges
- Standard Shipping: Free
- Express Shipping: $10 added to total

## Cart Rules
- Cart must contain at least 1 item before payment.
- Subtotal is displayed in element: #subtotal_amount
- Discount is displayed in element: #discount_amount
- Total is displayed in element: #order_total
